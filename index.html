Florida

Florida is a state in the southeastern region of United States.

Florida is the southernmost point in the USA. Technically Hawaii is the southernmost state in the country, however, continentally, geographically, Florida is the southernmost point. . The oldest inhabited city in the country resides in Florida. About 41 miles south of Jacksonville, St. Augustine stands as the oldest city in the US. Settled by Spaniards in 1565, the city is home to a 17th-century fortress.

Fun fact, Florida had the worlds first scheduled passenger flight in 1914 that flew fron St. Petersburg and tampa
