Miami

Miami is a city in Southeast Florida. Known for the beaches and culture!

City's Population: 439,890
Year Incorporated: 1896
Region: Southeast Florida
Classification: Urban
Average Income: $47,860 USD per Household
State's Income: $61,777 USD per Household

Miami is located in Florida, United States.
Miami is named after the Miami river.
Miami also knows for its solid Cuban influence and is the Capital of "Latin America."
Miami welcomes over 14 million tourists per year.

Fun Fact,Miami gets its name from one of the tribes that lived here, Mayaimi.
