Tallahassee

Tallahassee is the capitol of florida and home of the third tallest capitol building in the US.

Tallahassee is the capital city of Florida. Located in southeastern region of United States.
Became Florida's capital city in 1824
Tallahassee was founded in 1821 and the name Tallahassee comes from Muskogen indian word that means "Oil Field" or "Old Town".
Coldest places in the state during the winter

Region: Northern Panhandle
